PIONEER SCHOOL

Located in Maragua Sub County in the county of Murang’a, central Kenya, is the well-known Pioneer School Maragua. The school is quite popular despite its setting in the vicinity of the rural area of Maragua. The school is undoubtedly one of the best things to come from the sub-county. Pioneer School is a privately-owned boys’ boarding school that comes with great amenities and the promise of academic success.

Our Curriculum

Students follow the Kenya National Curriculum and are expected to sit in the KCSE exams. We place a deep curricular focus on STEAM (Sciences, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) and integrating technology into students' daily lives.
